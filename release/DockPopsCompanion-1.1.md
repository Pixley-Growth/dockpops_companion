## DockPops Companion 1.1

- Poplets now reuse the companion-managed live icon mirror instead of poking DockPops' protected containers directly, which fixes repeated permission prompts and generic fallback icons.
- Running poplets stay in sync more reliably during icon edits, organizer moves, refreshes, and relaunches.
- Refresh guidance in the companion now explains when a quick poplet relaunch is still needed after files and icons are synced.
